THE IMPACT

PrivacyButler gives activists in America the ability to freely express their opinions without the fear of being targeted or unlawfully suppressed. PrivacyButler offers activists a private channel to publish a variety of posts on social media, tools to obscure other activists’ or victims’ faces, and technology to hide personal contact information.



My process in the field:

THE CONCEPT

Through social media, where most activists speak up, PrivacyButler obscures faces in shared photos, shares posts and follows people anonymously, and reminds them to privatize their personal information on their social media accounts. It automates many security practices frequently ignored.

Designing for social activists
Who are frequently being targeted by the government simply because they hold opposing views. After talking to this group and participating in their events, I found that organizers of social movements are well protected, but their members lack proper protection. The members either do not put forth effort or are not aware of the methods of privacy protection.

Local Radio - An auditory sense of where you are.
When your airplane flies over a new area, local music will begin broadcasting.

Local Radio
When I interviewed potential users, many of them expressed their excitement when they visit new places. At the same time, they are fearful that they know nothing of the place they'll be visiting. Therefore, a radio station to play local music will help the user better connect to their environment through music.
