Bubble

Let's put an end to bullying.

Have you ever worried about your children being bullied? Have you ever worried you cannot understand what happened to your children? Bubble protects students from bullying and help parents and teachers understand what happen to children. By building a better communication between guardians and kids, kids can alert and report to guardians during or after an emergency. At the same time, we respect kids' privacy.

Mariana and I did secondary research, analysed bullying issues, and designed the solution in 24 hours. When we looked at the design problem, we decided to put the power of stopping bullying on kids. At the same time, parents and teachers will be notified and help protect kids. In this project, we focused on analysing the problem, come up with solutions, and some key interface design.
