Companionship

An interactive story-telling game on Sifteo Cubes.

“Companionship” is an interactive story project. The goal is engaging readers to accompany with people because companionship is a scarce resource in the modern time. By making it into Sifteo Cubes, players can put two cubes together to accomplish the “accompany.” People better engage with the story by having the action of putting together.
Project Advisor: Christina Wodtke

img of rendered finalized design

Why companionship?

This project required designers make people be willing to share a scarce resource. I took companionship as my topic because I was in a kind of sorrow and I want to help myself get rid of it. I interviewed 3 friends from different age and different culture background. I asked them to tell me a story of being helpless and upset and took sketch note. Interestingly, I found a pattern that all of them got rid of sorrow by talking to people. Both sharing their stories and listening to other stories help them move on. Therefore, I settled down with companionship.
